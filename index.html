Our New Project

At our core, we believe that every girl has the right to dream big and break through societal barriers. Our project is dedicated to challenging stereotypes that suggest women are less capable than men or cannot reach the highest levels of leadership.

We offer young girls the opportunity to create customise Barbie dolls that reflect their unique identities and aspirations. By playing with dolls that resemble themselves, girls can envision a future where they are confident, proud, and limitless in their potential.

Link with Charities

Moreover, all proceeds from the sale of these personalized dolls are donated to organizations that champion gender equality and empower girls. Some of these organizations include:

Plan International: An NGO focused on advancing children's rights and equality for girls.

Action Education: An organization working to ensure access to education for all girls and women, particularly in regions where women's rights are not upheld.

Our Other Initiatives

The Mattel PlayBack program

It allows for the recovery and reuse of materials from old Mattel toys, aiming for 100% recycled, recyclable, or bio-based plastic materials in products and packaging by 2030.

Barbie Diversification

Mattel expanded its Barbie line to include dolls representing individuals with Down syndrome and vision impairments.

Gender and Origin Equality

Mattel achieved 100% pay equity for all employees in every country, ensuring equal pay for similar work, regardless of gender or origin.

Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Mattel has implemented a strategy to reduce its greenhouse gas emissions, aiming for a 50% reduction by 2030 with 2019 as the baseline year.
